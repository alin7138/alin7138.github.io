I am an early career GIS professional currently based in Vancouver, BC. I spent my undergraduate degree conducting ecological research in the marshes of California, coral reefs in French Polynesia, and tropical forests in Panama. These experiences led me to focus on ways to map, monitor, and protect these landscapes. When I’m not making maps, I’m probably taking naps.
